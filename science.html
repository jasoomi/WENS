🌍 Science & Technology

🔬 Science

Science is the systematic study of the natural world through observation, experimentation, and reasoning. It seeks to understand the laws of nature and explain how the universe works.

📜 History of Science

Science has evolved over thousands of years, from ancient civilizations observing the stars, to the scientific revolution of the 16th–17th centuries, to modern-day particle physics and space exploration.

📚 Main Branches of Science

  • Physical Science – Physics, Chemistry, Astronomy
  • Life Science – Biology, Zoology, Botany
  • Earth Science – Geology, Meteorology, Oceanography
  • Formal Science – Mathematics, Logic, Computer Science

🧪 Scientific Method

  1. Observation
  2. Hypothesis
  3. Experimentation
  4. Analysis
  5. Conclusion

🌟 Importance of Science

Science improves our understanding of the world, drives innovation, and provides solutions for global challenges such as health, climate change, and energy.

💡 Technology

Technology is the practical application of scientific knowledge to create tools, machines, systems, and processes that solve problems and improve human life.

📜 History of Technology

From the invention of the wheel (~3500 BC) to the printing press (15th century), electricity (19th century), and the internet (20th century), technology has continually transformed society.

⚙ Types of Technology

  • Information Technology (IT) – Computers, Software, Internet
  • Medical Technology – MRI, Vaccines, Surgical Robots
  • Industrial Technology – Manufacturing, Automation
  • Green Technology – Solar panels, Wind turbines, Electric cars

🚀 Modern Applications

Today’s technology powers smartphones, AI, space travel, renewable energy, 3D printing, and blockchain systems.

🔮 Future of Technology

The future includes advancements in Artificial Intelligence, quantum computing, biotechnology, sustainable energy, and possibly human colonization of other planets.
