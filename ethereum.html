Ethereum At-a-Glance

Launched in 2015 by Vitalik Buterin, Gavin Wood, and other co-founders, Ethereum is an open-source platform enabling programmable blockchain, smart contracts, decentralized apps (dApps), NFTs, and DeFi.2

In September 2022, Ethereum transitioned from Proof-of-Work to Proof-of-Stake (“The Merge”), cutting energy usage by over 99%.3

Recent upgrades include “Dencun” (March 2024) introducing proto-sharding (EIP-4844), and the upcoming “Pectra” expected in mid-2025, which alters staking parameters per validator.4
